About

Passionate About Making a Difference

The journey of CWTS 1 has been really meaningful and an eye-opener. I had thought, at the beginning, that it was just another subject to be finished, but as time passed by, it helped me understand that CWTS is about service, discipline, and social responsibility. This will surely motivate students like me into being active citizens and create a love for community and the environment.

My activities have helped me understand the importance of serving others and of working in a group. Each activity has engaged us in how we should cooperate, communicate, and make a difference for betterment in small ways. Contributions to cleaning our community, awareness projects, or even the smallest acts of kindness make the society better and safer.

One thing I really appreciate about CWTS 1 is that it helps me discover my role as a student in nation-building. It teaches that real change starts with awareness and action.This subject doesn't just develop our skills; it even shapes our values and strengthens our character.

I want to grow into a more responsible, kind, and service-oriented individual as I go through life. I am reminded by CWTS 1 that helping others is a heartfelt commitment as well as a duty

I'll sum up by saying that CWTS 1 is about becoming a better version of ourselves, learning how to make a difference, one tiny act at a time rather than just learning.
